Henriqué Barcelos Jr

Creator • Storyteller • Faith-driven Voice

I’m Henriqué — a 23-year-old content creator and creative visionary from Johannesburg. I use my voice, my lens, and my platform to bring real stories to life. Whether I’m behind the mic on my podcast or creating videos that connect, everything I share comes from a place of authenticity, vulnerability, and purpose.

My work explores faith, mental health, and the raw beauty of healing. I believe in using creativity to build community — and in showing up with love, every time. This isn’t just content. It’s connection. It’s intention. It’s legacy in the making.

Let's build something that matters. You're not just watching a journey — you're part of it.
